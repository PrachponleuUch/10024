Job Reference Number: JRFA8056

Product Developer

  • Position Description Reference Number:

    J0001

  • Position Title:

    Full-time Product Developer

  • Brief Description:

    We are looking for a product developer to be responsible for creating and developing new products and improving existing products. The responsibilities of a product developer include conducting market research, collaborating with marketing, technical, and manufacturing specialists, and developing prototypes.

  • Salary Range:

    $AU75,000 to $AU90,000

  • The title of the position to whom the successful applicant will report:

    Product Development Manager

  • Key Responsibilities:

    1. Creating and enhancing products that fill a niche in the market or allow their clients to remain competitive
    2. Determining product specifications according to a number of various factors and uses
    3. Supporting manufacturing and design activities by developing prototypes for products in development
    4. Consulting market research materials and sales data to ensure that products are viable and competitive
    5. Submitting proposals and designs for evaluation by the project team and management before moving to the next stage.

  • Required qualifications, skills, knowledge and attributes:

    (Essential)
    1. Bachelor's degree in engineering or industrial design
    2. Be comfortable using prototyping equipment (3D printers and CNC machines)
    3. Be comfortable using design software (SOLIDWORKS and Autodesk)
    4. Proven working experience as a product developer or in a related field (3 or more years)
    5. Work well within a team
    6. Problem-solving skills.
    (Optional)
    1. Creative and innovative thinking skills
    2. Graduate Degrees.
Job Reference Number: JRFB8056

Software Developer

  • Position Description Reference Number:

    J0002

  • Position Title:

    Full-time Software Developer

  • Brief Description:

    Our company is seeking to hire a skilled software developer to help with the development of our current projects. Your duties will primarily revolve around building software by writing code, as well as modifying software to fix errors, adapt it to new hardware, improve its performance, or upgrade interfaces. You will also be involved in directing system testing and validation procedures, and also working with customers or departments on technical issues including software system design and maintenance.

  • Salary Range:

    $AU90,000 to $AU110,000

  • The title of the position to whom the successful applicant will report:

    Engineering Director

  • Key Responsibilities:

    1. Modifying software to fix errors, adapt it to new hardware, improve its performance, or upgrade interfaces
    2. Directing system testing and validation procedures
    3. Directing software programming and documentation development
    4. Consulting with departments or customers on project status and proposals
    5. Working with customers or departments on technical issues including software system design and maintenance
    6. Analyzing information to recommend and plan the installation of new systems or modifications of an existing system
    7. Consulting with engineering staff to evaluate software hardware interfaces and develop specifications and performance requirements
    8. Designing and developing software systems using scientific analysis and mathematical models to predict and measure outcomes and design consequences
    9. Preparing reports on programming project specifications, activities, or status
    10. Conferring with project managers to obtain information on limitations or capabilities.

  • Required qualifications, skills, knowledge and attributes:

    (Essential)
    1. Bachelor's degree in computer science or related degree
    2. Knowledge of the software development life-cycle
    3. The desire to work in a fast-paced environment
    4. Ability to develop unit testing of code components or complete applications
    5. Must be a full-stack developer and understand concepts of software engineering
    6. Experience working on a variety of software development projects
    7. Deep programming language knowledge
    8. 3 or more years of experience in similar jobs in this field.
    (Optional)
    1. Creativity is always a plus
    2. Graduate Degrees.
